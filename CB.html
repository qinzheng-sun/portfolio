This book was done over the course of three weeks with extensive research finalizing six main Chinese calligraphy types. Each calligraphy type was mapped out into six different categories with a cut-out shape introducing each category, making it more visually enticing as well as each cut-out shape is reflecting the integrity of its individual calligraphy.
