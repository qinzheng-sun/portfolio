The goal of SYM is to build an interactive website/platform for university students who may be struggling with stress and anxieties during their university studies and lives. Our website/platform serves the students who are more comfortable to speak out their issues online, and everyone has opportunities to reach out their minds through SYM. Therefore, SYM provides a platform for students to leave their stress and anxieties, but also communicate with other students who might have similar issues.
