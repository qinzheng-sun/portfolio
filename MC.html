Loneliness is a growing world epidemic. Many people are deciding to live on their own nowadays. Neighbourly relationships in big cities have become tense and exclusionary. Businesses are selling human interaction by the hour. Deep down, we all know it’s not good to spend time alone. But we do not always know how to break the bubble.

That is where Meal Chat comes in. Meal Chat is a digital application where it allows users to connect with people all over the world over food. Whether if you are having your lunch during break, or just simply having your meals at home, Meal Chat is there with you. This not only solve the problem of being alone or eating alone, but it can also help building connections with one meal at a time.
