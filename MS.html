Me, is a 12-inch by 12-inch cube sculpture, made of transparent mirror acrylic boards, foam sheets, coloured cellophane, Arduino LED strip and motion sensor. The concept of this work is about being closed off from the outside world, how an introverted person often feels. The exterior of the box is a representation of the human body, and the inner box is the heart and the light within, the core soul of a person.

With the help of kinetics, LED lights within the inner box adds life and motion to the sculpture. With a sensor also incorporated, the sculpture senses when something or someone is close by, the lights will dim and stop moving, and when the viewer is standing 2 meters or more away, the lights will continue to spin and shine. The sculpture can always be viewed from a distance, like an introverted person, shining their brightest when admired from far.
