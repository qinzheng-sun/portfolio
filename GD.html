Gestalt design emphasizes the importance of creating a clear and harmonious visual structure, where all elements work together to create a meaningful and cohesive image. This design approach is commonly used in posters to create a powerful impact and evoke emotions through simple, yet effective visual communication. By using basic design principles such as proximity, similarity, and continuity, I’ve created a series of eye-catching posters that based off of Gestalt’s design principal.
